Vision & Mission

Our calling

Our Vision

We are indigenous believers of Christ, bringing indigenous communities in the Philippines and abroad into a genuine relationship with God, establishing Christ-centered worship, discipleship, and mission.

Our Mission

To win influential indigenous people to Christ to genuinely encounter God, redeeming their God-given culture for Christian worship and evangelism.

Ministries

Ways to connect and serve

Indigenous Discipleship

Small groups, mentoring, and Bible training for leaders and families.

Worship & Prayer

Gatherings centered on Scripture, Spirit-led worship, and prayer.

Evangelism & Outreach

Community missions and Gospel sharing among indigenous communities.

Youth & Kids

Building a strong foundation of faith for the next generation.
