Church History

How God has been faithful since 1992

Solid Rock Ministries was established in 1992 in Baguio City, Philippines with a burden to reach and disciple indigenous people and leaders—so that entire communities may know Christ and worship Him with redeemed, God-honoring expressions of their culture.

Vogtareuth Outreach (Germany)

The Vogtareuth Outreach began in 2022 as a small Filipino group of believers gathering in Reuther Feld, Vogtareuth. What started with simple prayer, worship, and Bible sharing grew into a regular fellowship—strengthening believers and reaching friends and families with the love of Christ. Today, the outreach continues as a testimony that God can plant His work anywhere, using ordinary people with an extraordinary calling.

2022
A small Filipino believers’ group began meeting in Reuther Feld, Vogtareuth for prayer and Bible sharing.
2023
Fellowship grew in unity—regular worship, encouragement, and welcoming new friends and families.
Today
Continuing outreach in Vogtareuth—building faith, community, and mission in Germany.

What We Believe

We believe the Bible is God’s Word, salvation is through Jesus Christ alone, and the Holy Spirit empowers believers for holy living and mission.

  • Jesus is Lord and Savior
  • God’s Word shapes our life

Culture Redeemed

We use God-given culture and submit it to Christ—so worship and witness become much more meaningful, authentic, pure, and powerful for communities.

  • Honor what is God-given
  • Transform what is broken
  • Use culture for worship & evangelism

Vision & Mission

Our calling
