Church History

How God has been faithful through the years

Grace River Church began as a small home fellowship with a simple desire: to worship Jesus, grow in the Word, and serve our community. Over time, the Lord added people, leaders, and opportunities—forming a church family rooted in grace and guided by Scripture.

2015
A prayer group started meeting weekly in a living room.
2017
First public worship service held with children’s ministry.
2020
Expanded outreach, food support, and online services.
2024
Moved into a larger space and launched discipleship groups.

Vision & Mission

Where we are going and why we exist

Our Vision

To be a Spirit-filled church that transforms lives and communities by making disciples who live like Jesus and shine His love everywhere.

  • Raise strong disciples
  • Build Christ-centered families
  • Impact our city and nations

Our Mission

To worship God, preach the Gospel, disciple believers, and serve our community through practical love and faithful outreach.

  • Worship: honor God wholeheartedly
  • Discipleship: grow in Word & Spirit
  • Fellowship: belong and build one another
  • Evangelism & Compassion: share Jesus, serve people
