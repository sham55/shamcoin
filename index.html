Church History

Established 1992 • Baguio City, Philippines

Solid Rock Ministries was established in 1992 in Baguio City with a clear burden: to reach and disciple indigenous people and leaders, so that entire communities may know Christ and worship Him with redeemed, God-honoring expressions of their culture.

1992
Solid Rock Ministries established in Baguio City, Philippines.
1990s
Early outreach and discipleship among indigenous communities; leadership development begins.
2000s
Expansion of evangelism and training; partnerships and missions beyond local areas.
Today
Continuing the work in the Philippines and abroad—raising indigenous followers of Christ.

Vision & Mission

Why we exist and what we are building

Our Vision

We are indigenous followers of Christ, bringing indigenous communities in the Philippines and abroad into a genuine relationship with God—establishing Christ-centered worship, discipleship, and mission.

  • Indigenous followers of Christ
  • Indigenous communities reached locally and abroad
  • Communities transformed by the Gospel

Our Mission

To win influential indigenous people to Christ to genuinely encounter God, redeeming their God-given culture for Christian worship and evangelism.

  • Win influential indigenous people to Christ
  • Lead genuine encounters with God
  • Redeem culture for worship & evangelism
